Nesma Media & Entertainment Is Now Sarab Entertainment
Jeddah Solar Plant Project
APR 2021

An announced that the consortium led by Masdar and EDF Renewables, both global leaders in renewable energy, and Nesma Company, has reached financial close on a 300 MW utility-scale PV Solar Power plant in Jeddah and is starting construction.

The Renewable Energy Project Development Office (REPDO) of Saudi Arabia’s Ministry of Energy awarded the consortium the 300 MW Jeddah project after it had submitted the most cost-competitive bid.

Alongside the 400 MW Dumat al Jandal wind power farm currently under construction in the North of the Kingdom.
