4 Decades of
Consistent Growth

1979

STARTED IN

Nesma is an ongoing story of progress and growth that started in 1979. Each decade is a chapter marked by success and milestones.

14, February, 1979

Nesma is registered as a company.

1980’s
  • - Construction business launched.
  • - Operation and maintenance services started.
  • - Port management and marine services started.
  • - Telecom Australia partnership established.
1990’s
  • - Nesma becomes the largest internet service provider by market share in KSA.
  • - First company for Hajj and Umrah services acquired.
  • - Nesma becomes the biggest service provider for industrial projects by market share in KSA.
2000’s
  • - Franchise agreement for Jollibee Foods acquired.
  • - Support services business for oil and gas industry established.
  • - Community outreach projects in training and tailoring launched.
  • - Ferry business in Turkey began.
2010’s
  • - Airline business established in Egypt.
  • - Electrical equipment factory in Croatia purchased.
  • - Specialized construction-related training institute founded.
  • - Operations of container terminal at King Abdullah Port began.
  • - Community project to support fishermen launched.
  • - Nesma wins largest contract in company history to construct King Abdulaziz Road in Makkah.
2018

Company succession passed on to second generation.

2019

Nesma 40th anniversary celebrated.

2020

Nesma new strategy launched and new values unveiled.
