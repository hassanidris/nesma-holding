A LEGACY

His Excellence
SALEH AL-TURKi
FOUNDER & HONORARY CHAIRMAN

Nesma’s 40 years of impact were spearheaded by the vision, determination and leadership of His Excellency Saleh Al-Turki.

Al-Turki founded Nesma in 1979 with a vision to build an innovative Saudi company that meets the demands of the rapidly growing Kingdom.

He served as President and Chairman of Nesma until his appointment as Mayor of Jeddah in July 2018, under the leadership of King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud.
